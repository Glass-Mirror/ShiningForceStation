About

This site is current a work in progress. First published on 2018/3/24.


The purpose of Shining Force Station is to provide an easy place to go to for your Shining Force needs.

There are many other sites that serve a similar purpose though they have all their information spread around, each site having a bit of useful content. Furthermore, they have issues with their webhosts, preventing them from hosting certain types of content. This is not a problem for me, since I am my own host.


I'm currently a college student at Arizona State University. specifically, I'm working toward a Computer Science degree, with a concentration in cybersecurity.

This site has largely been a passion project. I loved this game as a kid, we had it on out Sega Genesis that we kept in the basement, and I was constantly playing it. Since then, I've been able to continue playing using virtual consoles, like on the Nintendo Wii and on Steam. And, so, I've made this site to help share a game that I love and enjoy, helping people learn about it and giving them resources for their own playthroughs.

As I said though, this site is currently under construction, meaning there will be times when it goes down for extended periods of time. Also as I am creating this site based on both my knowledge and that which I can find online, it is likely there will be some errors, in which case, please let me know so I can fix them.
